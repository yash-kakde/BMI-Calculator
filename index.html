What is BMI?

BMI is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

BMI Categories

Category BMI Range
Underweight Less than 18.5
Normal weight 18.5 - 24.9
Overweight 25 - 29.9
Obese 30 or more
